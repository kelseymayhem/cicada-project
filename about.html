Just like children everywhere, we were careless with our wild, beautiful, perfect youth. So on fire with everything that the magic we made became little more than coins left in a forgotten pocket. When we stepped apart, I don't think either of us realized that something irreplaceable was missing.

I guess if you're lucky to live long enough, even old, careless children can still find undeserved grace- and so we found ourselves together again. We had so much to tell each other and yet the time we spent seemed quiet. Our exchange was this- the music you are listening to. We met in our secret place and spoke only in our secret language. Every moment that passed between us lives in this work.

This music is the fruit of our intimate and unfettered creative conversation.

Thank you for listening. Thank you for letting us share. It's as honest as we can be.

Cicada Project / Brood X is the current work of Shandra Beri and Peter Hughes Snell.
